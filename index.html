About Me

Pivoting my career to chase my dreams

I moved to Houston, Texas in October 2022 from Boston, Massachusetts. I found a part-time job while I adjusted to the city and then a full-time job that utilized my biology degree. For almost a year, I grinded my day to day. I was not happy. I found no fulfillment in my work, I didn’t have any long term goals, and I knew the field was not for me.

I started coding because I wanted to make games. It started with a six week online course offered through Coursera and taught by the University of Colorado. Any free time I had after work I’d spend on my computer. I loved it. I decided to make the jump. After extensive research, I applied to a few post-college programs so that I could switch careers.

I was accepted into a Full Stack Program at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). The program is teaching me web development, front-end development with React, and back-end development with MongoDB, ExpressJS, and NodeJS so that I may become confident in the MERN stack. I am also learning essentials for working in the cloud, automated testing, and deployment for a solid foundation in DevOps.
